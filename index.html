Biography

I’m a master’s student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering department at Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey. My areas of interest include Software Engineering, Wireless Systems and Artificial Intelligence. Currently, I am working on my master’s thesis under the guidance of Prof. Dipankar Raychaudhuri at Wireless Information Network Laboratory - WINLAB.

Outside of research, I enjoy hiking, playing piano, and reading books.

Feel free to reach out!
